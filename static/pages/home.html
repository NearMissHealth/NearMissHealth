NearMissHealth seeks to borrow from the success of aviation safety by providing patients, family members, and caregivers a place to anonymously report errors and almost-error ("near misses") that lead to medical tragedies.

"... If my primary care doctor had referred me to an ear-nose-throat (ENT) specialist instead of brushing my condition off as just another pregnancy-related issue that would resolve on its own, I would have been treated for Sudden Onset Hearing Loss in the critical 24-hr window and may have regained my hearing. But by the time I realized I should have seen an ENT doctor, it was too late, and now I have permanent hearing loss in one ear."
- Patient in North Carolina
